Hobbies and Interests

Here is my list of personal interests and hobbies.

I like playing the guitar.

I have a Washburn X12B (I know, very basic electro guitar) with Seymour Duncan humbuckers (that's good :-)). I have several effects pedals. I have an acoustic guitar, but I don't like to play it. I can also play keyboard, but not that good as playing guitar


I like charcoal drawing.

In my spare time, I try to draw fantasy things (like in Forgotten Realms) and human silhouettes with small details.


I have a passion for cars.

I like car racing, especially drag racing. I also like driving cars, and I am a good driver.


I love playing FRP and RPG.

However, I haven't played by using pen and paper for a long time.


I love playing computer games, especially old and very old ones.

I did play several indie games, and I decided that I like the amateur soul in computer games. My favorite genre is action.


Sometimes I write small stories.

But I am not a really good writer.


I love reading comic books.

My favorites are the ones created in Italy. I read Marvel too.

I like traveling.

I always try to visit new places, when I find time for it.
